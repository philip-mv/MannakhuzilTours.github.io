Munnar Package

About Munnar

Munnar is a hill station blanketed with endless tea plantations, mist‑covered valleys and cool mountain air. It is perfect for travellers who love scenic drives, waterfalls and peaceful walks among rolling green hills.

This package can include sightseeing at Top Station, Echo Point, tea‑factory visits and photo stops at viewpoints. Stays range from cozy homestays to premium resorts, depending on your budget.

  • Ideal trip duration: 2–3 nights
  • Best time to visit: September – March
  • Customizable pickup from Kochi or nearby cities
