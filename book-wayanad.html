Wayanad Tour Packages – Mannakuzhil Tours

Discover the Wild Beauty of Wayanad – Kerala’s Green Mountain Escape

Wayanad is a lush hill district in the Western Ghats, known for dense forests, rolling hills, waterfalls and a cool climate. Our Wayanad tour packages are crafted for travellers who love nature, wildlife and scenic drives.

From peaceful stays in forest resorts to treks, viewpoints and off‑beat routes, Wayanad offers a refreshing break from city life for couples, families and groups alike.

Why Choose Our Wayanad Tour Packages?

  • Nature‑rich sightseeing: Viewpoints, dams, waterfalls, caves and forest routes.
  • Handpicked nature stays: Resorts, cottages and homestays surrounded by greenery and birdsong.
  • Adventure & outdoor fun: Short treks, plantation walks, jeep rides and optional wildlife safaris.
  • Great for families & groups: Flexible itineraries balancing relaxation with light adventure.
  • Easy transfers & support: Pickup, drop and local guidance from experienced drivers.

Highlights of Our Wayanad Packages

  • 2D/3D and 3D/4D Wayanad itineraries
  • Visits to viewpoints, waterfalls and forest areas
  • Tea/coffee plantation walks and village‑side drives
  • Pickup from Calicut, Mysore or nearby cities
